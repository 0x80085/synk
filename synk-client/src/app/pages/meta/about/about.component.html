What is this?

Viewers can watch videos together with other connected viewers in viewer-maintained channels.
Video content can be added from different video hosts (e.g., YouTube, Twitch) and the playback is synchronized for all the viewers in the channel.

Each channel has a chat feed for discussion and a playlist where users can queue up videos to play.

Currently only logged in users can enter channels and watch content but that may change in the future.

This project is intended to be open sourced once I deem it stable enough to release to the public.
